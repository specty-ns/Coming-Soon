    E-Plastic is a portal that includes three modules - Recycling Company, Plastic Collector, Customer. Where Customer can sell their useless plastic to Plastic Collector. The Plastic Collector collects that useless plastic and further sells it to Recycling Company. Recycling Company recycles that plastic and sells that through our website.

    By this, all (Recycling Company, Plastic Collector and Customer) can earn money while the environment is also saved.
